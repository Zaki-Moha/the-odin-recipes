Banana Smoothie

Whip up a rich and creamy banana smoothie in just 5 minutes with only 3 core ingredients:
frozen bananas, milk, and yogurt. It’s a perfectly quick, customizable, and filling breakfast or snack.

  1. In a blender, combine the frozen bananas, milk, and yogurt.
  2. Blend until smooth and creamy.
  3. Pour into glasses and serve immediately.
