Chicken Biryani

Enjoy this delicious and aromatic chicken biryani,
a flavorful rice dish layered with spiced chicken and fragrant basmati rice.

  1. Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. In a large bowl, mix together the chicken, spices, and yogurt.
  3. Spread the chicken mixture in a baking dish.
  4. Bake for 25-30 minutes, or until the chicken is cooked through.
