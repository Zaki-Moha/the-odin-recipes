Lasagna

Enjoy this classic Italian dish, layered with rich meat sauce, creamy ricotta, and melted mozzarella cheese.

  1. Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. In a large bowl, mix together the ground beef, onions, garlic, and tomato sauce.
  3. Spread the meat sauce in the bottom of a baking dish.
  4. Layer the lasagna noodles, ricotta mixture, and mozzarella cheese.
  5. Bake for 30-35 minutes, or until the cheese is bubbly and golden brown.
